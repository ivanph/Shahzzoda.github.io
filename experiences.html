Volunteering

Project Rousseau

Project Rousseau empowers youth in communities with the greatest need to reach their full potential and pursue higher education. They aim to do this with their four pillars: mentoring, academics, community service, and broadening horizons. I have led a class and have a wonderful mentee whom I hope to help as much as possible.

Magner Career Center

Magner Career Center is the career center at Brooklyn College, they offer resources for students to excel at their career and gain experience in their field. They have many useful workshops and events throughout the year. I have helped them out in many of these events and continue to do so whenever I can.

I also do one-two day volunteering for causes such as economic empowerment, animal welfare, community building, and others.

Extracurricular Activites

Co Director of WICS Mentorship Initiative

The intention of this program is to provide our members with a network of professionals in the field who have potentially faced the same challenges they are facing and build a support group to keep anyone who identifies as a woman to stay in tech and pursue a successful career. I did many aspects of mentor-mentee pairing, outreach to both, led orientations, and drafted the constitution to adopt the initiative into the WICS Club to ensure the longevity of the program.
